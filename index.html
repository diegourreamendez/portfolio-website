Research & Publications

Research Lines

Active Core Areas

Extreme Events

Characterization and quantification of peak discharge, extreme rainfall, and droughts using extreme value theory.

Multivariate Analysis

Modeling dependencies and joint risk of compound hydrological events using copulas and joint frequency distributions.

Non-Stationary Analysis

Assessing temporal trends and shifting parameters in hydrological time series induced by climate change.

Compound Events

Physical and probabilistic modeling of concurrent flooding drivers (fluvial, surge, and tides) in estuarine zones.
